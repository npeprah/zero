## Why Drawdown Matters

### **It Measures:**
1. **Account stability** - How much your account swings
2. **Emotional stress** - Seeing your account drop 22% is stressful
3. **Risk of ruin** - If drawdown is too big, you could lose everything
4. **Trading discipline** - Bigger drawdowns = less discipline

## Understanding "From Peak"

Important: **Drawdown is measured from PEAK, not from starting capital.**

Example:
```
Your account path:
  $25,000 (start)
  → $50,000 (peak)
  → $40,000 (trough)
  
Drawdown = $50,000 - $40,000 = $10,000
(NOT $25,000 - $40,000 = $15,000)
```

This is important because:
- You only care about losses from the highest point
- A loss from peak means "I gave back some gains"
- A loss from start means "I'm losing my capital"
