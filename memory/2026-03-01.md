### Core Risk Framework

**Sacred Rules (Non-Negotiable):**
1. Max risk per trade: 0.5-1% of account
2. Max daily loss: 2% of account → STOP TRADING if hit
3. GEX filter: Skip trades if GEX < 0 (prevents -40% to -60% tail days)
4. All positions closed by 3:30 PM ET (no overnight risk)
5. 90%+ stop execution discipline (exit immediately when triggered)
